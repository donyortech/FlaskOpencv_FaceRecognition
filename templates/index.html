About the Technology

How It Works

Using advanced algorithms, our system quickly and accurately identifies individuals from live video feeds...

Step 1: Real-time Capture

Our system initiates by capturing live video feeds, utilizing HD cameras to ensure high-quality image capture for precise recognition.

Step 2: Facial Detection

Advanced detection algorithms identify and locate faces within the video frame, even in crowded settings, to accurately pinpoint individual attendees.

Step 3: Identity Verification

Utilizing a pre-established database of authorized individuals, the system matches detected faces against known profiles, verifying identities with remarkable speed and accuracy.

Step 4: Attendance Logging

Upon successful identification, the system automatically logs the attendance, updating real-time records and providing instant feedback on the attendance status.
